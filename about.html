A bit about me

Hello! I'm Fionna, my pronouns are she/they, and I'm an aspiring product designer who seeks to foster empathy and positive change through user experience, and visual design. I grew up in Fort Smith, AR, and currently live in Evanston, IL, as a third year student at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Japanese.

I grew up as an art kid and always strive to combine my passion for visual art with creative problem-solving. My interest in issues such as accessiblity, social justice, and sustainability also guide my design approach to center inclusivity and functionality in problem solving.

Beyond school and work, I can be found on a long nature stroll, exploring new parts of the city, throwing clay on a pottery wheel, and gushing over thrift finds.
